Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute or until cooked.
  4. Let cool slightly and enjoy!

Fruit Salad with Honey-Lime Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1 cup diced mango
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Steps:

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Mix honey and lime juice in a small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over fruit and toss gently.
  4. Serve chilled or immediately.
