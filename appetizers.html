Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix with pancetta and egg-cheese mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.
